# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in LLM Field Notes a respectful,
curious, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
ability, background, identity, or level of experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and generous when giving feedback.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Make room for beginners and explain unfamiliar terms.
- Keep examples, documents, and bug reports free of private or sensitive data.
- Focus disagreement on evidence, trade-offs, and the work.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, threats, unwanted sexualized
attention, doxxing, and publishing private information without permission are
not acceptable. Deliberate disruption, plagiarism, or bad-faith manipulation of
the project’s feedback and knowledge graph are also out of bounds.

## Enforcement

Report a concern privately to the repository maintainers using the process in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Include what happened, where it happened, and any
relevant links or evidence. Reports will be handled as confidentially as
possible. Maintainers may remove content, limit participation, or suspend
contributors when necessary to protect the community.

This code of conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
